Frequently Asked Questions

Simple & Transparent Pricing. No Surprises. Lowest in Market.

Do I pay for cold boot time?

You only pay for the time that the hardware is available to used by your application, you will never pay for Cold Boot time.

Do you offer volume discounts?

If you are looking to deploy 50+ GPU instances, we offer volume discounts. Please fill out the Demo Form to discuss discounted pricing.

How do you have the lowest prices in the market?

We utilize unused, latent compute resources from gamers worldwide to power our network - think Airbnb for GPUs. The competitive marketplace helps keeps our prices low.

When do my charges start?

We do not charge for the initialization process (selecting hardware, downloading & loading container, etc). Once your container is running, charges start accruing.
